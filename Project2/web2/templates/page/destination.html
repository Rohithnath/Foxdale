DISCOVER JAPAN
DESTINATIONS

From the great white north to the sub-tropical south-and everywhere in between

Hokkaido

Japan's northern island is famous for its beautiful wilderness, world class powder snow, delicious seafood and fascinating indigenous culture. Just a short flight from Tokyo, Hokkaido is the perfect escape in all seasons

Tohoku

The Tohoku region of Japan is home to unspoiled rural landscapes and historical treasures, outdoor summer adventures and endless powder snow in the winter

Hokuriku Shinetsu

Mountains and sea meet in this wild region, and the result is sheer beauty

Kanto

Anchored by Tokyo, the world's biggest metropolis, Kanto also has rustic mountain getaways and gorgeous subtropical islands

Tokai

Served by the shinkansen between Tokyo and Kyoto, the Tokai region features both world-renowned destinations and local splendor

Kansai

There are many things to do in the Kansai region. Experience fantastic nightlife, peaceful temples and shrines, and soul-searching pilgrimages

Chugoku

For those in the know, the Chugoku region is full of natural beauty, rewarding hikes and great historical significance

Shikoku

Providing the stage for literary classics, fevered dancing, natural wonders and an ancient pilgrimage route

Kyushu

Easily reached by land, sea and air, dynamic Kyushu is bubbling with energy, culture and activity

Okinawa

Discover a distinct island culture born of subtropical sun, white sand, mangrove jungles and the age of the Ryukyu Kings
